Panchakarma is Ayurveda’s most powerful detoxification and rejuvenation therapy, designed to cleanse the body, balance the doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha), and restore natural health.

The term "Panchakarma" literally means "Five Actions", referring to five core therapeutic procedures that eliminate deep-seated toxins (Ama) from the body and mind.

Rooted in the ancient Ayurvedic scriptures like the Charaka Samhita and Sushruta Samhita, Panchakarma has been practiced for thousands of years as a path to long-term healing, prevention, and vitality. Unlike modern treatments that often only address symptoms.

Panchakarma aims to treat the root cause of disease by purifying the system and strengthening immunity.

The Five Main Panchakarma Therapies

Vamana

Therapeutic emesis (for Kapha imbalance)

Virechana

Purgation (for Pitta detox)

Basti

Medicated enemas (best for Vata disorders)

Nasya

Nasal cleansing (for head, sinuses, and stress)

Raktamokshana

Bloodletting (for specific skin and blood-related disorders)

These are supported by pre-treatment steps like Abhyanga (oil massage), Swedana (herbal steam), and post-treatment care involving balanced diet, lifestyle changes, and herbal support.
