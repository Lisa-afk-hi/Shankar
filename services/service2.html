Abhyanga – Full-Body Ayurvedic Massage

Abhyanga is a deeply relaxing full-body massage using warm, herbal oils tailored to your dosha (body type). Originating from Ayurvedic daily routines (Dinacharya), it stimulates circulation, detoxifies the body, and promotes long-lasting relaxation and vitality.

Price: ₹1200 – ₹2000 per session

Ideal For: Everyone – adults, elderly, and even children for immunity and growth

Benefits

Rejuvenates body and mind

Improves sleep and reduces fatigue

FAQs

➡ We use specially prepared medicated oils based on your dosha (body constitution). Common oils include Dhanwantharam, Ksheerabala, or herbal sesame oil blends.

➡ Yes, Abhyanga is safe and beneficial for children. It improves circulation, boosts immunity, and supports healthy growth when done with mild pressure and appropriate oils.

➡For general wellness, once or twice a week is ideal. During seasonal changes or for health issues, a daily regimen may be advised.
