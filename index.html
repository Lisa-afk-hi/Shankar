Shree Gyaneshwar Math Trust

Shree Gyaneshwar Math Trust is a Charitable Trust established in the year 1912. The great Sanyashi Late Swami Gyananand Maharaj ji was the founder of this trust. The Trust is now headed by Shree Mhamandaleshwar Swami Shankaranad Saraswati.

Shri Jnaneshwari Trust established Shankara Ayurvedic Center with a vision to promote the health and well-being of people through the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda & Yoga

Rooted in the ancient sciences of India, our mission is to provide natural, effective, and holistic healing solutions that nurture the body, mind, and soul.
